This repository marks the beginning of my second research project, which started in Summer 2025 and continues into the present. The research focuses on building Foam-GPT: a fine-tuned large language model workflow for generating OpenFOAM case files used in computational fluid dynamics simulations.

The work introduced me to practical LLM fine-tuning across several frameworks. I learned how to use LLaMA-Factory for faster experiment configuration, TRL for lower-level supervised fine-tuning control, and Unsloth for memory-efficient training. I also worked with LoRA, PEFT, and quantization to make model adaptation more practical under GPU memory limits.

This stage of the research involved preparing datasets, configuring model runs, comparing training behavior, and understanding the difference between lower training loss and actual usefulness in OpenFOAM file generation. It gave me the foundation for later evaluation, deployment, failure analysis, and dataset improvement work.
