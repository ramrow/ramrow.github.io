This paper presents FoamGPT, a fine-tuned large language model for agentic automation of CFD simulations with OpenFOAM. It was published in the NeurIPS 2025 Machine Learning and the Physical Sciences Workshop.

The paper describes how OpenFOAM tutorial cases can be turned into a specialized dataset for training an input-writer model. The model is designed to generate OpenFOAM case files as part of a larger simulation automation workflow, connecting LLM fine-tuning with practical CFD setup tasks.

This publication represents the research outcome of the Foam-GPT work: dataset creation, supervised fine-tuning, model evaluation, and integration into an agentic OpenFOAM workflow.
